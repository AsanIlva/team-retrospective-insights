  • Breaking down large tasks into smaller, manageable sub-tasks improves progress visibility.
  • Early agreement on a mini style guide can save significant time in later stages.

  • Develop and share a brief project style guide at the outset.
  • Continue using a Team Health Monitor with regular checkpoints.
